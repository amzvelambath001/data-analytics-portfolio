Instagram Performance
Analysis

Prepared by Amritha VR
Date May 2026
Tool Used Excel / Google Sheets
Posts Analyzed 10 posts
01

Business Question

"Our Instagram engagement has been dropping for 3 months. What content type and posting time drives the best performance?"

02

Data Used

10 Instagram Posts
3 Content Types
Likes, Comments, Saves tracked
Reach per post tracked
Engagement Rate calculated
03

Key Findings

📈
Highest Reach
Reels
Average reach of 3,850 per post — 3× more than Photos
💬
Highest Engagement Rate
Photos
18.3% avg engagement rate — outperforming Reels (16.3%) and Carousels (16.0%)
🕐
Best Posting Time
Afternoon
Afternoon posts averaged 17.1% engagement vs 16.6% for morning posts
⚡
Surprise Finding
Photos beat Reels on engagement
Despite lower reach, Photos outperformed on engagement rate — challenging the common assumption that Reels always win
04

Content Type Breakdown

Content Type Avg Reach Avg Eng. Rate Reach (relative)
Reels 3,850 16.3%
Carousels 2,733 16.0%
Photos 1,117 18.3%
05

Recommendation

Goal: Brand Awareness
Post more Reels in the afternoon
Reels deliver 3× the reach of Photos. Afternoon timing shows marginally higher engagement. Best strategy for growing new audiences.
Reels · Afternoon
Goal: Community Engagement
Post Photos for deeper connections
Photos achieve the highest engagement rate (18.3%) despite lower reach — indicating a loyal, highly interactive audience segment.
Photos · Any Time
